Clearing Cookies and Browser Cache

To resolve performance lag or visual bugs in the main dashboard:
1. Open Google Chrome.
2. Click the three dots menu at the top-right corner.
3. Select 'Delete browsing data...' or press Ctrl+Shift+Delete.
4. Set the Time Range to 'All time'.
5. Check the boxes next to 'Cookies and other site data' and 'Cached images and files'.
6. Click 'Delete data' or 'Clear data'.
7. Refresh the page to reload assets.
