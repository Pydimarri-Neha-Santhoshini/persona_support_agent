Subscription Billing and Refund Policy

1. Billing Cycle
Subscribers are billed on a recurring monthly cycle starting from the day of signup. For example, if you register on June 15, your card will be charged on the 15th of each subsequent month.

2. Duplicate Charges
If you notice duplicate charges on your statement, it is typically due to temporary authorization holds. If two finalized charges appear for the same billing date, contact the finance team immediately.

3. Refund Requests
Refunds are eligible only if requested within 7 days of the initial subscription charge. Additionally, API credit usage must be below 10% of the monthly quota. Refund requests can be initiated by emailing billing@adsparkx.com.
