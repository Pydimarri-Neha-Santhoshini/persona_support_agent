Email Notification and SMTP Configuration

If notifications or invitation emails are not reaching users, verify the following:
1. Default SMTP Server Host: mail.smtp.adsparkx.com on port 587.
2. Secure connection setup requires TLS.
3. Verify that the SPF (Sender Policy Framework) and DKIM keys are correctly published on your DNS manager.
4. Ensure target addresses are not placed on the global unsubscribed bounce list.
